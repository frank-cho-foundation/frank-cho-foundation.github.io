This conference had gathered the scholars, civil rights lawyers and political activists from both China and the U.S. to discuss the issue faced by the war crime redress movement. Scholars presented their latest researches on Japan’s war crime issues and discussed the memory issues in Japan whereas the lawyers explained their strategies in making the law cases for wartime victims’ compensation.
